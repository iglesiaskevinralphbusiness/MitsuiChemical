Evolue™
metallocene linear low desity
polyethylene

Evolue™ is C6-LLDPE made from the perfect combination of Metallocene catalyst and Mitsui Bimodal process technology.

Evolue™ demonstrates superior strength, clarity and heat-seal properties compared to conventional-catalyst LLDPE. This unique product provides an exceptional balance of physical properties and process-ability. Evolue™ is used extensively in the manufacturing of various flexible packaging materials.
