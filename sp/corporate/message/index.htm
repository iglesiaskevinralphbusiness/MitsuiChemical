Director's Message

Shigeharu Matsuzaka
Managing Director & CEO

Mitsui Chemicals Asia Pacific, Ltd. (MCAP) is committed to contribute to the Growth of Asia Pacific Region.

MCAP is the Asia Pacific Regional Headquarter of Mitsui Chemicals Inc., covering ASEAN10, South Asia, Nations along the Gulf in Middle East and Oceania.

MCAP is fully committed to contribute to Asia Pacific region, providing solutions to social issues through our products and services for a better tomorrow of the region.

Mitsui Chemicals Group in Asia Pacific region has 25 Affiliates for various products such as Metallocene Polyethylene, Polypropylene Compounds, Elastomers, Phenol, PTA, PET resin, Polyurethane materials, Adhesives, Coating materials, Agrochemicals, Nonwovens, Packaging Films, Industrials Films or Functional Sheets for a variety of industries of Mobility, Food & Packaging, Healthcare or Basic Materials. Also Mitsui Chemicals Singapore R&D Centre and MCAP Technical Support Centre have been dedicated to create advanced technology innovation and excellent technical services in the region.

The Key role of MCAP is being the Asia Pacific growth functional hub to drive the business growth, providing robust functional services to regional affiliates or businesses, delivering values to customers leveraging full power of Mitsui Chemicals Group.

Mitsui Chemicals Group would like to be your best partner for the resolution of social challenges through our business activities based on our excellent technologies, products or expertise keeping in harmony with the global environment.

Sincerely yours,

Shigeharu Matsuzaka
Managing Director & CEO
