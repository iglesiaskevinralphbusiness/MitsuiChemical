Internship Program

The Mitsui Chemicals, Inc. Internship Program is to give undergraduates an opportunity to experience practical work in the chemicals industry in an overseas environment, to encourage undergraduates to join the Chemicals industry after graduation, and to give undergraduates an opportunity to experience the Mitsui Chemicals work culture, and to deepen the relationship with Mitsui Chemicals Group of Companies upon graduation.

Eligibility

The Internship Program is open to Undergraduates of all nationalities. Applicants must be full-time undergraduates in their third year of studies, from chemical-related fields in the Science faculty and Engineering faculty of the National University of Singapore (NUS) / Nanyang Technological University (NTU).

Where Tenable and Internship Period

The internship will be held at one of MCI production sites or R&D centers in Japan. The internship period is for 10 weeks.

Value of Internship

An internship allowance of 3,000 JPY per day, free accommodation at MCI dormitory inclusive of utilities, breakfast and dinner, commuting expenses between dormitory and workplace, round-trip airfare between Singapore and Japan to take up the internship, and other necessary expenses for participation in MCI events.
