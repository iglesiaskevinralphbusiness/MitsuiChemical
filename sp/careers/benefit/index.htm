Rewards & Benefits

Our employees work together as a team to meet our business goals. In turn, we value our employees, encourage their career development and reward their performance.

We are committed to train and develop employees to their fullest potential. It involves encouraging and assisting all employees to strive continuously for improvement in their jobs and to upgrade their skills so as to meet challenges in the ever-changing business environment.

Our Benefits

We offer employees a wide range of benefits.

  • Flexible Benefits where employees can choose a basket of benefits, items that cater to individual needs, e.g Optical expenses, Holiday Subsidy, Purchase of computers etc.
  • Group insurance for Surgical & Hospitalization, Term Life and Personal Accident Insurance
  • Maternity/Marriage/Paternity/Compassionate Leave
  • More than 2 weeks of Annual Leave
  • Medical and Dental Reimbursement
  • Yearly Health Check-up
  • Maternity Benefits
  • Congratulatory Gifts
  • Service Award
  • Training & Education Assistance

We believe you should enjoy the benefits of a healthy work-life balance. That is why we actively host a series of activities such as family day, karaoke nights and sports events
